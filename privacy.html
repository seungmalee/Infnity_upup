Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Noendrise processes only the information needed to run the game, save progress, and provide online features.

Information We Process

We may process the player ID, country code, highest floor, kills, gold, lives, play time, chat messages, and online connection status. We do not collect account passwords, payment information, government identification numbers, or other sensitive personal information.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide online play, show rankings, support chat, restore game records, prevent abuse, and keep the service stable.

Ads and Cookies

This site may display ads after Google AdSense approval. Third-party advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits. Users can manage personalized ads through browser settings or Google ad settings.

Retention

Ranking and gameplay records may be stored for as long as needed to operate the service. Chat and temporary connection data may be retained or reset depending on service operations.

Contact

Privacy-related requests can be submitted through the contact page.
