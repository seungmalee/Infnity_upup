Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Noendrise processes only the information needed to run the game, save progress, provide online features, understand site performance, and keep the service stable.

Information We Process

We may process the player ID, country code, current floor, best floor, kills, deaths, gold, lives, selected title, selected cosmetic, play time, chat messages, online connection status, and basic request information such as IP-derived rate-limit data. We do not collect account passwords, payment information, government identification numbers, or other sensitive personal information.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide online play, show player and country rankings, support chat, restore game records, prevent abuse, enforce rate limits, debug service issues, measure site performance, and keep the game stable.

Chat and Public Rankings

Player IDs, country codes, chat messages, and ranking records may be visible to other players. Do not submit private personal information in your player ID or chat messages.

Analytics, Ads, and Cookies

This site may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic and gameplay page performance. This site may display ads after advertising partner approval. Third-party advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve and measure ads.

Google explains how it uses data from partner sites and apps at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. Users can manage personalized ads through browser settings and Google ad settings.

Retention

Ranking and gameplay records may be stored for as long as needed to operate the service. Chat and temporary connection data may be retained or reset depending on service operations, abuse prevention, or maintenance needs.

Children's Privacy

Noendrise is a general-audience game and does not intentionally request personal information from children. If you believe personal information was submitted by a child, please contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Contact

Privacy-related requests can be submitted through the contact page.
