Fair Play Rules

Noendrise is built for quick competition, readable chat, and honest ranking attempts.

Expected Behavior

  • Use player IDs that are readable and not impersonating another person, brand, or service.
  • Keep chat short, game-related, and respectful. Harassment, hate, threats, sexual content, and spam are not allowed.
  • Do not attempt to overload the server, automate score submission, or manipulate ranking requests.
  • Report bugs or suspicious ranking behavior through the contact page instead of abusing the issue in live play.

Ranking Integrity

The server applies rate limits, input cleanup, and progress caps to reduce obvious abuse. These protections are not a promise that every score is perfect, but they help keep the ranking board useful for normal players. Records that appear to come from abuse, automation, or server manipulation may be reset.

Advertising And User Experience

Advertisements, if enabled after approval, should never block the climb controls, hide important ranking information, or force accidental clicks. The game remains playable without clicking advertisements.
