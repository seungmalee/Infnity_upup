Noendrise Items Guide

Items add timing, risk, and comeback potential to every Noendrise run.

Boulder

Boulder is useful when opponents are below you or close enough to be pressured. It can stun lower players and create space while you keep climbing.

Missile

Missile targets a player above you. Use it when another climber is pulling away or when you need a chance to slow the current leader.

Warp

Warp moves you up by 100 floors. It is strongest when you want to reach the next milestone, escape crowded floors, or recover after falling behind.

Shield

Shield blocks one danger while active. Save it for crowded areas, high floors, or moments when another player is likely to attack.

Hide Reset

Hide Reset refreshes your hide cooldown. It helps you prepare an ambush, avoid a missile, or safely pause during a risky climb.
