Noendrise Rankings Guide

Noendrise rankings turn each climb into a global player and country competition.

Player Rankings

The player leaderboard highlights climbers by their best floor and related run records. A higher floor is the clearest way to improve your rank.

Country Rankings

Country rankings group performance by country code. Choosing a country lets your progress contribute to a broader global competition.

Best Floor and Time

Your best floor shows how high you have climbed. Run time can help compare strong runs when players reach similar milestones.

Kills, Gold, and Lives

Attacks, gold rewards, and lives are part of the competitive loop. Smart item use can protect your run and improve your overall contribution.

Ranking Tips

  • Focus on consistent correct movement before chasing risky item plays.
  • Use Shield before crowded or dangerous floors.
  • Save Warp for milestones or recovery moments.
  • Watch both player and country tabs to understand the current competition.
