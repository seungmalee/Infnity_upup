Noendrise Game Guide

A practical guide for climbing higher, surviving longer, and understanding the live ranking system.

Basic Goal

Your goal is to climb as many floors as possible. The current floor, lives, gold, kills, items, and best record are shown in the game HUD. A run can end when penalties or attacks push you back to 0F, but your best floor and unlocked progress remain part of your profile.

Controls

  • Climb: use the main movement control to advance one floor at a time. On mobile, use the on-screen controls.
  • Items: item slots are numbered. Use an item when the timing matters, especially near opponents or after a long combo.
  • Hide: hiding can protect your position, but it has a cooldown. Use it before danger, not after you have already lost control.
  • Chat: the live chat is for short game messages, score reactions, and bug reports during play.

Items And Effects

Items are designed to change the pace of a run. A warp can push you upward quickly, a shield can block a dangerous moment, and attack items can pressure players above or near you. Because item timing is limited, strong players usually save items for milestone floors, crowded ranking races, or recovery after a stun.

Rankings

Noendrise has both player rankings and country rankings. Player ranking focuses on individual best floors and run times. Country ranking groups performance by selected country code, so even casual players can contribute to a national score by pushing their personal best higher.

Strategy Tips

  • Do not pause too long outside safe areas. Idle pressure is one of the main survival checks.
  • Rest floors are useful moments to read the ranking board, choose a title, or plan item usage.
  • Gold is most valuable when it changes a run outcome. Spending too early may leave you without a recovery option later.
  • Watch the live leader. If the top player is nearby, an item can be more valuable than raw climbing speed.
