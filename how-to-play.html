How to Play Noendrise

Climb as high as possible, react to the next stair direction, and use items at the right moment.

Basic Controls

  • Desktop: use Left, Up, and Right arrow keys to climb.
  • Desktop: press Down to hide.
  • Desktop: press 1, 2, or 3 to use an item slot.
  • Mobile: use the on-screen arrow buttons and hide button.

Goal

Choose the correct stair direction and keep climbing. A wrong direction can end the current run and send you back to 0F. Higher floors unlock titles, items, and stronger ranking scores.

Items

  • Boulder can stun lower opponents.
  • Missile targets a player above you.
  • Warp jumps you up by 100 floors.
  • Shield blocks one danger while active.
  • Hide Reset refreshes your hide cooldown.

Rankings

Noendrise includes player rankings and country rankings. Your best floor, speed, kills, gold, and country contribution can all matter depending on the leaderboard view.
