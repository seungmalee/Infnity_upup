Noendrise FAQ

Common questions about the Noendrise online stair climbing game.

Is Noendrise free to play?

Yes. Noendrise is free to play in a modern web browser.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. The game includes on-screen controls for mobile browsers, including climb buttons and a hide button.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Noendrise runs in the browser, so you can play without installing an app.

How do rankings work?

Noendrise shows live player rankings and country rankings. Your best floor and other gameplay records help define your position.

Who should I contact about bugs or privacy?

Use the contact page for bug reports, advertising inquiries, and privacy requests.
